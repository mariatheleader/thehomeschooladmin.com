My Story

During her career as an early childhood educator in childcare and Head Start programs before becoming a nanny, Maria found her morals and values challenged often.

In 2019, after learning about vaccine mandates forced on Maine students, she focused much of her energy on advocating for medical freedoms and rights. Eventually, she founded The Homeschool Admin in response to the growing need for family-centered homeschool support.

She answered the calls from her local community to offer her education, experience, and expertise as a service for homeschooling families.

Asking God, “What do I have to give the world, and what does the world need?” she was led to shape her gifts into her true calling.
