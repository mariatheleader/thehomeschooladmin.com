About

You love the idea of homeschooling. But no one told you it would also require being a curriculum director, scheduler, and administrator.

I'm Maria. I've been helping families build homeschools that actually work since 2017—not just a pretty binder or a color-coded planner, but a real system that runs in your real life.

I work with mothers who are educating multiple children with different ages, different needs, and different learning styles. The overwhelm isn't weakness. It's the reality of doing something that most people have no idea how to set up correctly.

How It Works

    Application & Discovery

    You fill out a detailed intake so I can understand your children, your values, your schedule, and what's not working. This is the foundation everything is built on.

    I build a complete plan based on your family—what to use, what to let go of, and how to structure your days, weeks, and year in a way that actually holds.

  3. 03

    We set up your system together. You don't have to figure out how to put it into motion—that part is handled.
