Hi, I'm Maria

After working with homeschooling families for 5 years, I've found the biggest challenge for homeschool parents is the added layer of constant decision making.

Most homeschool parents can find information. The harder part is knowing what applies to your child, your home, your schedule, and your real life right now , and what to do when things stop working.

I hold a Master's in Leadership and a Bachelor's in Child & Youth Development. I've worked alongside 55+ families , preschool through high school , helping them build routines that actually hold, choose curriculum that fits their child, and make confident decisions without the spiral.

Whether you're just starting out, hitting a wall, or simply want someone who knows child development in your corner: I'm here.
