Frequently asked questions

For parents searching for real homeschool help.

Do I need to prepare before booking?

No. You do not need to prepare before booking. Bring the questions, screenshots, links, confusion, fear, and open tabs. We will sort through it together.

Can you help me choose curriculum?

Yes. Curriculum help is one of the clearest reasons to book. I can help you compare options, think through your child’s needs, avoid overbuying, and decide what makes sense for your home.

Can you help with preschool homeschool?

Yes. Many parents are told to “just let them play,” but still want a gentle rhythm at home. We can create a developmentally aligned plan that gives you structure without pressuring your child too early.

Can you help with homeschool high school transcripts?

Yes. I can help you think through credits, transcripts, diploma planning, alternative electives, resumes, and how to align the high school years with college, career, or post-graduation goals.

Can I book if I am brand new to homeschooling?

Yes. New homeschoolers are a perfect fit. We can walk through what matters first, what can wait, what you need to know, and what you can stop overthinking.

Do you meet in person in Southern Maine?

Yes. In-person sessions are available in Southern Maine when scheduling and location make sense. We can meet at a library, coffee shop, or agreed local space.

What is the difference between Telegram and a call?

Telegram is best when you want flexible support across a week. A 90-minute call or in-person session is best when you want focused attention and faster decision making in one sitting.

Is this ongoing?

No. Each option is a one-time payment. You can book another session later if you want more support.

Will you tell me exactly what to do?

I will give you clear guidance, recommendations, and next steps while still honoring your child, your family values, your home rhythm, and your role as the parent.
