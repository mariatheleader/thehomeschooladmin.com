Yes — and that is exactly what the Clarity Room gives you. Maria is a Child Development Specialist with a Master's in Leadership. She is not just another homeschool parent. She is the expert you have been looking for.

Who can help me figure out how to homeschool my specific child?

Maria can. She asks about your child, your family rhythm, your curriculum, your schedule, and what is actually happening in your home — then gives direction that fits your real life, not a generic template.

Can I get help with curriculum and scheduling?

Yes. You can ask about curriculum choices, daily routines, weekly structure, learning styles, documentation, and what to change when something is not working. This is exactly what the room is for.

How do I know if I am homeschooling correctly?

This is one of the most common questions. Maria helps you see what is working, what is not, and what to do next — so you can stop spiraling and move forward with real confidence.

How many hours a day should I homeschool?

Many families homeschool effectively in 1–3 hours a day with the right rhythm and structure. Maria can help you figure out what makes sense for your child's age, stage, and your family's current season.

Can I homeschool if I work from home?

Yes. Many parents in the Clarity Room work while homeschooling. The key is building a realistic structure and knowing what actually needs your attention — Maria helps you make those decisions.

What if I need more than weekly support?

Some families need their full homeschool system built and planned — that is what ORGANIZE and CONFIDENCE are for. Maria can help you figure out if the Clarity Room is the right fit or if a deeper offer would serve you better.
