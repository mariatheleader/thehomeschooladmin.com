Common Questions

Things families ask
before they apply.

I'm not sure I need "all of this." Can I just get a little help?

That's the most common thing I hear — and it usually means THRIVE is exactly right. The Extensive Support pace is designed for families who just need a system built and then space to run it themselves. You're not signing up for constant hand-holding. You're building something once that works all year. Most families are surprised by how light it feels once the system is in place.

We've already started homeschooling. Is it too late to join?

Not at all. We can start at any point in the year — beginning, middle, or end. Families who are already homeschooling often make the most progress quickly because they know exactly what isn't working. We build from where you are.

What if I'm not in Southern Maine?

All four sessions are available virtually for families anywhere. Southern Maine families have the option of in-home sessions, but virtual sessions are just as effective — many of my long-term families have worked with me entirely over video call.

How is this different from hiring a tutor or joining a co-op?

A tutor works with your child. I work with you — the parent. THRIVE is about building the system behind your homeschool: the routine, the tracking, the documentation, and the confidence to lead it yourself. A co-op replaces your teaching. This makes your teaching better.

My kids are very different ages. Can this work for a mixed-age household?

Yes — this is actually one of THRIVE's strengths. Mixed-age households are one of the hardest things to manage alone, and building a system that works for multiple children at different levels is exactly what the household system design session is for. The 3–5 children plan was created specifically for this.

What happens after I apply?

Once you submit your application, I'll reach out via Instagram or the contact info you share to have a quick, low-pressure conversation about what your family needs and whether THRIVE is the right fit. If it is, I'll send over the contract, payment link, and everything to get started. If not, I'll point you toward something that might serve you better.
